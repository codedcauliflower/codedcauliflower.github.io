FACTIONS

NCR

The New California Republic, a democratic nation struggling to uphold ideals of old in the unforgiving Mojave, maintains a strong military presence in the region. Though their intentions are noble, the Republic's thirst for land and resources puts them at odds with Mr. House's regime and Caesar's Legion.

Caesar's Legion

Caesar's Legion, a brutal, imperialistic slaver society, seeks to conquer and enslave the tribes of the Mojave Wasteland. Modeling itself after the ancient Roman Empire, the Legion upholds a totalitarian dictatorship under the ruthless command of Caesar.

Mr. House

Led by the reclusive trillionaire Mr. Robert House, the House faction aims to rebuild civilization by transforming New Vegas into an independent, technologically-advanced city-state under House's autocratic rule.
